## Legal & IP Summary

Price elasticity modelling and dynamic pricing theory are foundational to mature mathematical practices with no known active patents. Revenue management disciplines (from airline and hospitality industries) are established practices. EU Omnibus Directive compliance and MAP/MSRP constraints are regulatory and brand governance requirements, not IP concerns. GDPR and ePrivacy frameworks govern customer-data usage for personalised pricing but do not encumber the technology itself. No IP barriers identified.
